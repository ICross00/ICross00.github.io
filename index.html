Instructions
- Familiarize yourself with the button layout before pressing start.
- When ready, press the 'Start' button. The experiment will begin when you click the first black square.
- The grey button indicates which button will turn black next.
- When the test starts, click the black buttons as they appear as fast as possible until no more buttons remain.
- Wait until your score has been recorded to receive further instructions.

Please remember that this is a test of how the hardware device facilitates your interaction with the computer, and not a test of your skill.
